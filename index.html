Megumi & Tsugumi


Synopsis


The "Megumi & Tsugumi" series is located inside the omegaverse world. In this romantic story, it portrays the famous plot of "enemies to lovers". Tsugumi, intially in a different school, enrolls to a school with Megumi, as they start their whole romantic highschool life together. Just like any other highschool relationships, they face issues and struggles that challenge the strength of their romantic bond. Will they stay together till the end of graduation?
